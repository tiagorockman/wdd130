Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about the different types of rafting trips that are available in the area. The website will also provide information about the different companies that offer rafting trips, as well as the different prices and packages that are available. The website will also provide information about the different safety precautions that are necessary when rafting.

Audience

The target audience for this website is people who are interested in learning more about rafting. The website will also be a valuable resource for people who are looking for a rafting trip to take.
